# How We Verify Sellers

ZingLots is a curated marketplace. Every seller must pass **KYB (Know Your Business)** checks before listing. This protects buyers, honest sellers, and the marketplace as a whole.

## What we verify (KYB)

- **Business identity:** legal name, address, tax/EIN (or equivalent)  
- **Owner/representative:** government-issued ID and match to business  
- **Contactability:** working email and phone; post-sale contact method  
- **Payouts:** bank account on file (via Stripe)  
- **Inventory legitimacy:** sample listings or proof of possession/agency

> We may request additional documents for higher-risk categories or large volumes.

## Badges & what buyers see

- **Verified SME** — Passed KYB and approved to sell.  
  _Buyers see:_ Seller name, city/state, badge tooltip, rating (when available), response time, pickup policy, and a link to the seller’s profile with other active auctions.

- **Pro Seller** — Consistently strong performance.  
  _Typical thresholds (subject to change):_  
  – ≥ 20 lots/quarter, **on-time pickup ≥ 95%**, **dispute rate ≤ 2%**, responsive within 1 business day.  
  _Buyers see:_ “Pro Seller” badge and performance metrics on the profile.

## Listing standards

- Accurate titles and specs (brand, model, dimensions, power, year if known)  
- Honest condition grades; disclose defects and missing parts  
- At least **4 clear photos** (front, detail, label/plate, any defect)  
- Clear **pickup window** and location city/state; optional delivery/freight notes  
- No prohibited items (counterfeit, unsafe, hazmat without compliance, stolen goods, etc.)

## After a sale

- Seller receives order details after buyer payment.  
- Seller and buyer coordinate pickup or delivery directly.  
- Provide reasonable assistance for loading if promised in the listing.  
- Mark the order **picked up** when complete.

> ZingLots does **not** operate a carrier or guarantee shipping. Logistics are set by the seller and agreed with the buyer.

## Disputes & performance

- If a buyer reports “not as described” within the window, we’ll request evidence from both sides and decide per our [Disputes](/legal/disputes) policy.  
- Repeated issues (no-shows, misrepresentation, late pickups) can result in badge removal or account suspension.

## How to become a Verified or Pro Seller

1. **Apply:** [Start Selling](/apply) — complete the form with business details.  
2. **Upload docs:** We’ll request what’s needed for KYB and payouts.  
3. **First listings:** Your first few lots may be reviewed before going live.  
4. **Maintain performance:** Hit the Pro thresholds to earn/keep the badge.
