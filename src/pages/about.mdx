## Why businesses use ZingLots

- **Verified sellers**  
  Every seller passes our business verification (KYB) before listing.

- **Fair, transparent auctions**  
  No hidden fees. Clear buyer’s premium and card processing shown up front.

- **Extended bidding (anti-snipe)**  
  Last-minute bids extend the timer so everyone gets a fair shot.

- **Fast, secure payments**  
  Payments handled by Stripe; sellers receive payouts quickly after a sale.

- **Tax-ready receipts**  
  Buyers can download invoices/receipts for bookkeeping.

## How auctions work

1. **Browse & watch** lots by category or city.  
2. **Bid with confidence.** If a bid arrives in the final minutes, the close time extends automatically (anti-snipe) so no one loses on a last-second refresh.  
3. **Pay securely.** Complete checkout online within the stated payment window.  
4. **Coordinate pickup or delivery directly** with the seller. Sellers define their pickup windows and, if they offer delivery or freight assistance, they’ll state it in the listing. _ZingLots itself does not provide shipping services._

## Who we serve

- Facilities managers handling relocations or remodels
