# Non-payment & Pickup Deadlines

**Payment window:** Pay within **24 hours** of auction close unless stated otherwise.  
**Pickup window:** Complete pickup within the **window stated by the seller** (typically 3–7 days).

**If you can’t make it:** contact the seller before the deadline to arrange an alternative. Storage/handling fees may apply if disclosed.

**Missed deadlines:** lots may be forfeited and relisted; your account can be limited. Repeated issues lead to suspension.
